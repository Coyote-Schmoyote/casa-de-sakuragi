About us

We are a small international team of two running a world-class exclusive micro-cafe based in Tokyo, Japan. Our guests can enjoy our original Casa de Sakuragi recipes from all over the world, while admiring the breath-taking piece of nature amidst the charming Shitamachi area of the old Tokyo from our all-year-round-open outdoor terrace.

Outdoor Terrace

One of the main reasons (apart from the superb cuisine, of course!) our guests love Casa de Sakuragi, is our spectacular views. The lively Arakawa river can be seen at any time of the year, but there are some hidden seasonal gems as well! To name a few, our guests can enjoy the cherry blossoms in spring, and Mt. Fuji view in autumn!
